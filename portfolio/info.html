About me

My name is Michelle, and I’m a designer, creative, & storyteller. I’m currently a junior studying Journalism & Asian Languages and Cultures and pursuing the Segal Design Certificate at Northwestern University.

I love learning new things and picking up various skills along the way (a self-proclaimed lifelong learner). As someone who has dabbled in a range of experiences, I bring an interdiscplinary skillset to every project I undertake and draw on my creativity to amplify diverse stories.

In my free time, I like to journal the trash I’ve collected & add books to my neverending tbr (to be read) list.
